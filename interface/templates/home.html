ABOUT US

Chambua is an online platform that seeks to enable schools and learning institutions to obtain hidden information about student perfomance by analysis of exam results.Through the use of modern tools and analytics techniques,Chambua provides a clear, cut through view of what lies beneath.

We provide a breakdown of exam results in a way no other platform does, bringing the strengths of analytics into the education sector.From rankings to creative visualizations on data, our aim is to give learners more understanding of their abilities.
